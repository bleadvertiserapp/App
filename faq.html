What is BLE Advertisement?

+

BLE Advertisement is an advanced Android application that allows you to simulate and broadcast Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) advertisements. It's designed for developers, testers, and IoT enthusiasts who need to test BLE scanners or simulate BLE devices.

What are the minimum requirements?

+

Requires Android 8.0+, BLE capable device, and location permission (Android requirement for BLE advertising).

How do I create my first BLE profile?

+

Tap "+" ‚Üí Choose Custom or predefined template ‚Üí Configure settings ‚Üí Save ‚Üí Toggle ON to advertise.

What's the difference between Legacy and Extended Advertising?

+

Legacy: 31-byte packets, works on all BLE devices
Extended: Up to 251 bytes, requires BLE 5.0

What automation triggers are available?

+

Manual, Duration, Schedule, Location (Geofencing), WiFi, Battery/Charging, Motion, and Boot triggers.

üîß Troubleshooting

Why isn't my advertisement showing up on scanners?

+

Check: Bluetooth ON, Location permission granted, Location services enabled, Profile toggle ON, Packet doesn't exceed size limits.

Advertising stops when I close the app. How do I keep it running?

+

Disable battery optimization for BLE Advertisement and don't dismiss the notification.
